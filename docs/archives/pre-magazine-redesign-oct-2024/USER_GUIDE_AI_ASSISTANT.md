# Your Personal Guide - How to Use the AI Assistant

## What Is This?

Think of it as having a patient, thoughtful friend who helps you explore housing options at your own pace. This isn't a salesperson or a pushy agent - it's just someone who listens and asks good questions to help you think things through.

## How to Start a Conversation

### On Computer
1. Look for the **round button with a message icon** in the bottom-right corner of your screen
2. Click it
3. A chat window will open with a greeting
4. Type whatever is on your mind
5. Press Enter or click the arrow button to send

### On iPad or iPhone
1. Look for the **round button** at the bottom of your screen
2. Tap it
3. The chat window opens - it fills most of your screen
4. Tap the text box and type
5. Tap the arrow button to send

## What Can I Talk About?

Anything related to your housing situation:

- **Your current situation**: "I'm thinking about moving..."
- **Your concerns**: "I'm worried about affording a new place"
- **Your needs**: "I have trouble with stairs"
- **Your feelings**: "I'm not sure I'm ready for this"
- **Your family**: "My daughter thinks I should move"
- **Your questions**: "What should I be thinking about?"

## Tips for Best Experience

### Take Your Time
- There's no rush
- You can pause the conversation anytime
- Come back to it later if you need a break
- Think about your answers - this isn't a test

### Be Honest
- Share what you're really feeling
- It's okay to say "I don't know"
- You don't have to have all the answers
- The guide is here to help you think, not judge

### Ask for Breaks
If you're feeling overwhelmed, just type:
- "Can we pause this?"
- "This is a lot to think about"
- "I need to take a break"

The guide will understand and give you space.

## Common Questions

### Q: Is my conversation private?
**A**: Yes. Your conversation is saved only on your device (your computer or iPad). Nobody else can see it unless they use your device.

### Q: Will someone contact me because I used this?
**A**: No. This is just for you to think things through. Nobody will call or email you unless you specifically request help from a realtor.

### Q: What if I make a mistake typing?
**A**: That's completely fine! The guide understands typos and casual language. Just type naturally.

### Q: Can I use this on my phone?
**A**: Yes! It works on iPhones, iPads, Android phones, and tablets. Just tap the message button to start.

### Q: Does it cost anything to use?
**A**: No. This is completely free for you to use.

### Q: What if I close it by accident?
**A**: Just click the message button again. Your conversation will still be there.

### Q: Can I print the conversation?
**A**: Not yet, but that's a feature we're working on! For now, you can take screenshots if you want to save something important.

## Examples of Good Conversations

**You**: "I don't even know where to start."

## If Something Goes Wrong

### The chat won't open
- Try refreshing the page (press F5 or ‚åòR)
- Try a different browser (Chrome or Safari usually work best)
- Make sure your internet is connected

### The guide isn't responding
- Wait a moment - sometimes it takes a few seconds
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page

### The read-aloud feature isn't working
- Make sure your volume is turned up
- Try using headphones
- Check if your browser is blocking audio

### I don't like how it's responding
- Click "Clear" to start a new conversation
- Try being more specific about what you need
- Remember: it's okay to say "This isn't helpful right now"
